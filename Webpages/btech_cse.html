B.Tech CSE

B. Tech. (Bachelor of Technology) in Computer Science and Engineering is a program offered by the School of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. This program focuses on the study and application of advanced machine learning techniques, neural networks, natural language processing, and robotics to develop intelligent systems that can simulate human-like decision-making and problem-solving. Graduates of this program are prepared for careers in various industries where artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies are in high demand.
